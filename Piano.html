You can play the piano with the mouse or your computer keyboard

Click key or Press 7 to see what letter on the computer keyboard correspond to the piano keyboard.

E/R = Enter or Return, BD = backspace or Delete, Sft = shift, Sp = Space, Up = Up Arrow, Dn = Down Arrow, left = Left Arrow, Right = Right Arrow

Click key again or Press 3 to go back to the original piano keyboard
